Biography

Tanvinur Rahman Siam is a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Port City International University (PCIU), Chattogram, Bangladesh. His work spans Machine/Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Biomedical Informatics, HCI for Smart Classrooms, and Big Data Analytics. He enjoys mentoring students and collaborating on applied AI research that creates measurable impact in education and healthcare.
